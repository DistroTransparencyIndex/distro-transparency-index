Important Notice: DTI Update in Progress

We are currently in the process of gathering additional information from various Linux distributions to enhance the accuracy and completeness of our Distro Transparency Index (DTI).

The final update of the DTI will be published on September 9, 2024.

This deadline allows ample time for distributions to respond to our information requests and for our team to thoroughly analyze and incorporate the new data. The updated index will reflect the most current state of transparency across Linux distributions.

We appreciate your patience and invite you to check back on or after September 3, 2025, for the most up-to-date and comprehensive DTI rankings.

Thank you for your interest in promoting transparency in the Linux community.
