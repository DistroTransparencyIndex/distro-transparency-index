Join the DTI Board

Help Shape the Future of Linux Distribution Transparency

The Distro Transparency Index (DTI) is seeking qualified individuals to join our board for the 2025 index. As a board member, you'll play a crucial role in evaluating and promoting transparency in Linux distributions.

Who We're Looking For

We're seeking experts in various fields related to open source, transparency, and technology governance. Ideal candidates will have:

  • Extensive experience in open source software, technology policy, or related fields
  • A strong commitment to transparency and open governance
  • No current affiliations with specific Linux distributions to ensure impartiality
  • The ability to dedicate time to board activities (approximately X hours per month)
  • A track record of collaborative work and ethical conduct

Available Roles

We're looking to fill the following positions:

  • Open Source Security and Privacy Expert
  • Academic Specialized in Free and Open Source Software
  • Open Source Governance Expert
  • Legal Professional Specialized in Open Source Licenses
  • Linux Community Representative (unaffiliated with specific distributions)
  • Organizational Transparency Expert
  • Technology Journalist or Open Source Industry Analyst

Selection Process

  1. Submit your application using the form below
  2. Initial review by the DTI founding team
  3. Interviews with shortlisted candidates
  4. Final selection based on predefined criteria
  5. Public announcement of the new board members
