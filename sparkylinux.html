Final Score: 20.59/100

Concluding Analysis

SparkyLinux demonstrates limited transparency and accessibility across most of the evaluated areas.

Strengths:
  • Excellent accessibility of source code.
  • Minimal commercial influence on governance.
  • Some level of community participation encouraged through forums.
Areas for Improvement:
  • Formal governance documentation and transparency in decision-making processes.
  • Economic transparency and accessibility of financial information.
  • Development of a public roadmap and more structured code review processes.
  • Increasing community participation and documenting development processes.
  • Establishing formal control and balance mechanisms in governance.

The score of 20.59/100 reflects SparkyLinux's limited formal transparency structures. While it performs well in code accessibility and maintains a community-driven approach, significant improvements are needed across most evaluation criteria.

As a lightweight Debian-based distribution with multiple editions, SparkyLinux appears to prioritize functionality and usability. However, increasing transparency and formal governance could strengthen the project's position and long-term trust within the open-source community.
