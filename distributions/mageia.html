Concluding Analysis

Mageia demonstrates an exceptionally high level of transparency and accessibility across all evaluated areas:

  • Excellent governance transparency with detailed statutes.
  • Strong economic transparency and accessibility.
  • High accessibility of source code and development processes.
  • Detailed public roadmap and transparent code review processes.
  • Decentralized governance structure with minimal commercial influence.

The score of 97.06/100 reflects Mageia's outstanding commitment to transparency and openness. This exceptionally high score demonstrates Mageia's dedication to community-driven development and open governance.

It's important to note that Mageia's history as a community fork of Mandriva Linux likely contributed to its strong focus on transparency and community involvement from its inception. The project's structure, with its non-profit organization status and elected council, provides a strong model for transparent and community-driven distribution governance.
