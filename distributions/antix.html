Concluding Analysis

antiX demonstrates varying levels of transparency and accessibility across the evaluated areas.

Strengths:
  • Excellent accessibility of source code.
  • Minimal commercial influence on governance.
  • Moderate community participation in development.
Areas for Improvement:
  • Formal governance documentation and decision-making transparency.
  • Economic transparency and accessibility of financial information.
  • Development of a public roadmap and more structured code review processes.
  • Establishment of formal control and balance mechanisms in governance.

The score of 29.41/100 reflects antiX's limited formal transparency structures. While antiX performs well in source code accessibility and maintains a community-driven approach, there are significant areas for improvement across most evaluation criteria.

antiX, as a lightweight, systemd-free distribution with a strong ideological position, may prioritize technical and usability goals over structured governance. Nonetheless, implementing transparent practices would strengthen its standing and trust within the open-source ecosystem.
