Concluding Analysis

ArcoLinux demonstrates varying levels of transparency and accessibility across the evaluated areas.

Strengths:
  • Excellent accessibility of source code.
  • Minimal commercial influence on governance.
  • Moderate community participation in development.
Areas for Improvement:
  • Formal governance documentation and transparency in decision-making processes.
  • Economic transparency and accessibility of financial information.
  • Development of a public roadmap and more structured code review processes.
  • Decentralization of decision-making power and formal control mechanisms.

The score of 26.47/100 reflects ArcoLinux's limited formal transparency structures. While the project does well in code accessibility and offers educational value, formal governance and transparency could significantly improve trust and long-term sustainability.

As a project with a strong educational focus and diverse editions, ArcoLinux may prioritize user experience and learning over governance formalities. However, implementing transparent practices could further strengthen its reputation in the open-source ecosystem.
