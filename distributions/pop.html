Concluding Analysis

Pop!_OS demonstrates varying levels of transparency and accessibility across the evaluated areas.

Strengths:
  • Excellent accessibility of source code.
  • High transparency in code review processes.
  • Some level of community participation in development.
Areas for Improvement:
  • Formal governance documentation and decision-making transparency.
  • Economic transparency and accessible financial information.
  • Development of a clear public roadmap.
  • Reduce System76's opaque control or improve transparency about their role.
  • Establish stronger control and balance mechanisms.

The score of 26.47/100 reflects Pop!_OS’s limited formal transparency structures. While it excels in code accessibility and review openness, most other transparency pillars require serious improvement.

It is worth noting that Pop!_OS is developed by System76, a hardware manufacturer. This relationship often prioritizes product integration and user experience over open governance, and while understandable, it affects transparency. Clarifying this balance would improve public trust and the project’s standing in the open-source community.
