Final Score: 23.53/100

Concluding Analysis

Vanilla OS demonstrates varying levels of transparency and accessibility across the evaluated areas.

Strengths:
  • Excellent accessibility of source code.
  • Minimal commercial influence on governance.
  • Some community participation encouraged through GitHub.
Areas for Improvement:
  • Formal governance documentation and decision-making transparency.
  • Economic transparency and accessibility of financial information.
  • Development of a more comprehensive public roadmap.
  • Structured and transparent code review processes.
  • Increased community participation and documentation of development processes.
  • Formal control and balance mechanisms in governance.

The score of 23.53/100 reflects Vanilla OS’s limited formal transparency structures. As a relatively new project, it is understandable that some governance and transparency mechanisms are still evolving.

Vanilla OS focuses heavily on technical innovation, such as its immutable root filesystem and custom package management. This emphasis, while valuable, may be currently prioritized over establishing formal open governance practices. Future transparency efforts could greatly enhance its standing in the open-source ecosystem.
